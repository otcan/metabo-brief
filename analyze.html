Analyze a raw genotype file in your browser.

MetaboBrief parses 23andMe-style and AncestryDNA-style SNP files locally, matches rsIDs against the starter panel, and renders an evidence-attributed report.

Files are read with browser APIs. The default app has no upload endpoint.
