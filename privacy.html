MetaboBrief

Privacy

Privacy Notes

MetaboBrief is a static starter. It does not include a backend, account system, analytics, or data upload service.

What we collect

  • The demo stores only cookie-banner preference in local storage.
  • No genotype, metabolomics, or health data is collected by this static template.

Data handling guidance

  • Do not publish real personal health, genotype, or metabolomics data in a public fork.
  • Use synthetic examples or properly consented, de-identified datasets.

Storage and processors

The included demo has no third-party form processor. GitHub Pages and the CDN assets you keep may still receive normal web request metadata.

Your choices

If you deploy your own version with analytics, forms, uploads, or account features, replace this page with your own privacy policy.

Contact

Open source project discussion happens through GitHub issues.

We use essential cookies and local storage to remember your preferences. We do not use advertising or analytics cookies.
