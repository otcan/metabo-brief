How We Develop Your Report

From raw SNPs to ranked constraints and clear next steps.

Grounded science. Clear translation.

1
Variant Mapping

We start with your raw SNP file.

  • We identify variants that affect metabolic enzymes, transporters, and regulatory proteins.
  • We ignore cosmetic or ancestry-only variants.

Only functionally relevant biology is considered.

2
Pathway Impact Assessment

Instead of looking at genes in isolation, we map them into metabolic pathways.

We evaluate:

  • Where capacity may be reduced
  • Where flux may be inefficient
  • Where compensatory load may increase

This prevents overinterpreting single SNPs.

3
Constraint Prioritization

Not all variants matter equally.

We rank potential bottlenecks based on:

  • Functional evidence
  • Pathway centrality
  • Redundancy in the network
  • Cumulative effect

You receive only the top constraints most likely to influence real physiology.

4
Translation to Action

Each constraint is translated into:

  • A biological explanation in plain language
  • Directional recommendations
  • Suggested validation markers

We do not diagnose disease. We provide structured hypotheses.
